## Editor's Note

Source material this week is thin on vendor announcements, benchmark disclosures, and CVEs, so the usual sections are absent rather than padded with speculation. What the community has surfaced, however, are two tools worth keeping on your radar if you work regularly with DynamoDB and infrastructure automation.

## Worth Reading

[MechCloud](https://mechcloud.io) — A stateless alternative to Terraform aimed at infrastructure provisioning workflows, relevant for teams managing DynamoDB table configurations and IAM policies at scale.

[DynamoSQL](https://dynamosql.com/) — A SQL interface layer for DynamoDB, offering a familiar query syntax over a non-relational data model for teams that find the native API or PartiQL integration insufficient for their access patterns.
